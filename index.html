Map Features

  • Layers: Toronto Census, Toronto Red Light Cameras, Toronto Benches, Toronto Road Topology, 3D Buildings
  • Layer Selection Menu: Users can select the layer desired for information.
  • 3D Buildings: Displays 3D buildings.
  • Pitch Control: Allows to rotate camera view by right click hold and drag.
  • Zoom-in Color Change: As you zoon in to the street level you notice the color change in the buildings at various zoom levels.
  • Navigation: Zoom-in and zoom-out buttons.
  • Map Legend: Population Legend
  • Pop-Ups: Users can click the bench to get bench address
